ChameleonUltra compatibility

This app outputs raw NTAG215 .bin files (540 bytes) — exactly what the ChameleonUltra GUI expects. No conversion needed.

  1. Download a dump (single amiibo or Export all).
  2. Open the ChameleonUltra GUI / client.
  3. Load the .bin into an MFU or NTAG slot.

Dumps are read from your own amiibos. Files stay in your browser (IndexedDB) and are never uploaded.
